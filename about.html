Who We Are

More Than Just HVAC

Ray's Heating & Cooling LLC was founded with a single principle: treat every customer like a neighbor — because in Bridgeport, they are. For over 20 years, we've been the go-to HVAC contractor for homeowners and businesses throughout Fairfield County.

What started as a one-truck operation has grown into a full team of licensed, factory-trained technicians. But we've never lost the personal touch that separates us from the big chains. Every call is treated with urgency, every job is done with care.

We strictly follow manufacturer specifications and back all our work with some of the strongest guarantees in the industry. When we leave your home, your system runs right — or we come back until it does.
